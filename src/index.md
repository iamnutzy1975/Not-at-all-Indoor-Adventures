Welcome to **Not At All Indoor Adventures**. We provide premier fishing guiding in the Nipigon region of Northern Ontario. This isn't a "day at the lake"—it's a pursuit of legendary fish in one of the most powerful ecosystems on the planet.

Whether you're chasing the descendants of the world-record Brook Trout or vertical jigging for "Grey Ghosts" (Lake Trout), we provide the expertise to get you on the bite.
