# Professional Equipment

We don't let gear failure ruin a once-in-a-lifetime fish.

* **The Boat:** 20ft Lund Pro-V, powered by a 225HP Mercury. Built to handle the unpredictable "inland sea" of Lake Nipigon.
* **Electronics:** Humminbird HELIX 12 with Side Imaging and LakeMaster mapping.
* **Rods/Reels:** St. Croix Legend Tournament series paired with Shimano reels.
* **Safety:** Full USCG-approved safety equipment and satellite communication for remote areas.
