# The Giants of the North

The Nipigon region is a unique intersection of cold-water habitats. Because we have access to the Nipigon River, Lake Nipigon, and Lake Superior, we can target a variety of trophy species throughout the season.

---

## Select a Species to Learn More:

### The Legends
* [**Brook Trout (Specks & Coasters)**](/species/brook-trout)  
  *The World Record genetics. Specializing in trophy coasters and river specks.*
* [**Lake Trout (Grey Ghosts)**](/species/lake-trout)  
  *Deep-water monsters. We vertical jig and cast the shoals for 20lb+ fish.*

### The Predators
* [**Northern Pike**](/species/pike)  
  *Massive "Gators" in the weedy bays of Lake Nipigon. 40-inch class fish.*
* [**Walleye (Yellow Gold)**](/species/walleye)  
  *The ultimate shore lunch fish. Plentiful and aggressive in the river system.*

### The Chrome & The Stream
* [**Steelhead / Rainbow Trout**](/species/rainbow-trout)  
  *The spring and fall acrobats of the North Shore tributaries.*
* [**Brown Trout**](/species/brown-trout)  
  *The elusive masters of the Superior shoreline.*
